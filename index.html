Catastrophic — 1 to 19

7Bonnie's body begins to dissolve into shadow. She has 3 rounds to end the effect by succeeding on a DC 18 Constitution saving throw each round. If she fails all three she becomes incorporeal permanently until a 9th-level Remove Curse is cast on her.
8Every corpse within 1 mile animates and begins moving toward Bonnie. They don't stop until destroyed or until Bonnie moves more than 1 mile away.
9The ground in a 40 foot radius around Bonnie becomes a dead zone. All creatures that die within it cannot be resurrected by any means for 100 years.
10Time stops for everyone except Bonnie and her allies for 1 minute. Bonnie and her party can move and act freely. All other creatures are frozen. Should any damage be dealt or taken, the effect ends. When time resumes, no one else is aware anything happened.
11Bonnie's shadow detaches and goes berserk for 1 minute. It attacks everything in range using her spell attack modifier and deals 4d10 necrotic per hit. It has her hit point maximum. She cannot control or communicate with it.
12A DC 16 Charisma saving throw radiates from Bonnie in a 30 foot radius. Anyone who fails is convinced they are already dead for 1 hour. They take no actions. They don't respond to anything. They just wait.
13Every light source within 500 feet is permanently destroyed. Not suppressed — destroyed. Magical light sources must be recast. Torches are ash.
14The spell Bonnie casts works perfectly. Then The Fate's voice says her name from somewhere that has no location. Every creature within 60 feet that hears it must succeed on a DC 17 Wisdom saving throw or be frightened of Bonnie for 24 hours.
15Bonnie ages 30 years instantly. Her power ages with her — all her spell save DCs and spell attack modifiers increase by 3 for 1 hour, after which she returns to her normal age.
16A wave of pure necrotic energy expands from Bonnie in a 50 foot radius. Every creature must make a DC 20 Constitution saving throw, taking 8d10 necrotic damage on a failed save or half on a success. Bonnie absorbs all damage dealt as temporary hit points.
17All party members gain advantage on all saving throws until the end of their next turn. Bonnie takes 4d10 necrotic damage as the protective energy tears through her.
18The spell fires at its intended target and at Bonnie simultaneously.
19Ravens fill the sky above wherever Bonnie is standing. Thousands of them. They blot out the sun for 1 hour in a 1 mile radius. During this time all undead within the area have advantage on all rolls.

The Raven Queen — 100
100The wild magic stills. Every hostile entity within 60 feet must make a DC 22 Constitution saving throw or die instantly, their life force pulled into Bonnie's hands like thread. For each creature that dies this way, Bonnie may choose one ally and restore them to full hit points. Around Bonnie, every raven within a mile lands and goes silent.
Roll a d100 whenever Bonnie casts a spell and the DM calls for it.
Below 20, the First God stirs. At 100, her mother watches.
